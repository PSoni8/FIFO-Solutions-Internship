WOMEN OWNED BUSINESSES GET 10% OFF WITH US!

March 8, 2021

It’s International Women’s Day! And as seriously unsung heroes when it comes to agriculture to hospitality/ food & beverage product industries, we wanted to take a few minutes to talk about the fearless & formidable females that are redefining food-based industries here in Texas to across the world!

In the US:
+35% of farmers are female
+ 20% of women are chefs
+ 18% of women are executives in food-based companies

While still the minority in food production, we are so excited to be a company that supports the growth of careers for females as they support industries sustainable growth and value-based production.

Today we are so happy to get to celebrate the females that make our company great, beyond their daily commitment to their communities and drive to define the future of their work. The women behind Texas Olive Ranch make sure our quality is always the focus. A huge shout out to Maria and her amazing female-focused team in our bottling and shipping warehouse, Bethany Jones with Sojourner Creatives, the leader of her brilliant eye & visual creative direction, & Chelsea Miller with Form Follows Studio, for her leadership in marketing. 

For women who are working hard at running their own food-based businesses, we are launching a discount for you this month! Take 10% off your order & enjoy free shipping by using the code WOMENWORKIT. If you want to talk about our bulk products for your business, our VP Josh Henry would love to work with you directly to meet you and your business needs. Go ahead & give him a contact at josh@texasoliveranch.com
