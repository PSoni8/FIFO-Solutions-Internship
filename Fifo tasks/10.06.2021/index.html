SAP on Azure

Microsoft Azure runs your reliable mission-critical SAP workloads on a scalable and compliant platform with enhanced user experience and can create identity integration requirements and single sign-on. Organizations can also experience a three-year ROI of above 100 percent by migrating the legacy SAP infrastructure to Azure.
Suneratech, with its innovative Azure services , enables improved business outcomes providing predictive insights. Our expert cloud solution helps you simplify innovation, collaboration, and transformation to run SAP’s core business processes. Get partnered with us and benefit from Azure-SAP integrations.

Our Value Proposition

  • Zero Cost Cloud Migration to Azure — Move your SAP workloads to Azure at zero upfront investment with our Zero Cost Cloud Migration (ZCCM) program. We’ll invest upfront in both Azure BOM (on behalf of the customer) and Migration Services. The customer pays us from the savings realized after the migration has been successfully completed.
  • No-cost Azure Migration assessment report and consulting services, leveraging Block 64 (www.block64.com), a Microsoft-approved agentless tool.
  • No-cost Rapid & Complex proof of concepts, to ensure safe and secure migration.
  • No-cost “SAP S/4 HANA move workshop,” leveraging “S4Express”. S4Xpress is a Suneratech platform that can accelerate your S/4HANA migration by 50% and reduce cost by 30%.
  • SaaS-based automated regression testing, leveraging “CloudTestr”. CloudTestr is a Suneratech platform that can reduce the cost of SAP testing by 60 to 80% and 4X quicker cycles.
